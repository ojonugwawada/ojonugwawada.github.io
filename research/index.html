📄 Featured Publications

  • Revolutionizing AgriTech: Integrating Automated Machinery for Sustainable Farming Practices (2023) This study explores how mechanized farming can sustainably transform rural agriculture in Nigeria. Conducted across 30 farms between 2019 and 2021 with Goldvest Nigeria Limited, the findings show a 32% net profit increase using machinery. While cultural concerns and maintenance costs hinder adoption, younger farmers show openness to change. Human-Systems Interaction (HSI) frameworks proved critical in aligning technology design with user realities, supporting sustainable growth and farmer empowerment. 📄 View Research PDF
  • Transforming Agricultural Pest Management: The Effect of Artificial Intelligence and Remote Sensing (2025) This research examines how AI and remote sensing technologies are reshaping pest control in agriculture. Emphasizing precision farming, drone imagery, and IoT sensors, the study highlights how early pest detection and predictive analytics can reduce crop loss and pesticide use. Despite their promise, high costs and limited access pose challenges for smallholders. The study advocates for inclusive tech development, capacity-building, and interdisciplinary collaboration to promote sustainable and scalable adoption. 📄 View Research PDF
  • Design and Development of a Decision Support System for Predicting Student Academic Performance (2024) This study uses machine learning to predict student performance based on behavior, lifestyle, and mental health factors. It applies regression models on a dataset of 1,000 students, identifying study hours, sleep, and emotional well-being as top predictors. Linear Regression achieved the best accuracy (RMSE: 5.14, R²: 0.90). An interactive prediction tool was also developed to support educators and parents in making data-driven interventions. The research advances educational data mining and personalized academic support systems. 📄 View Research PDF
